BrightCoffee aims to maximise revenue and improve overall sales performance across its stores. This project analyses transactional sales data to uncover key trends, customer behaviour patterns, and product performance insights.

The analysis focuses on identifying factors that influence revenue, including seasonality, time-based sales patterns, and product demand, while also highlighting opportunities for business growth and optimisation.

🎯 Objectives
Analyse monthly sales trends to identify seasonal patterns
Evaluate sales performance by time intervals (Morning, Brunch, Daytime)
Identify top-performing and low-performing products
Measure key business metrics such as revenue and transaction volume
Provide data-driven recommendations to improve performance
📊 Key Insights
📅 Monthly Trends
Sales increase significantly during May and June
This aligns with the winter season, where demand for hot beverages rises
Seasonal demand plays a major role in revenue growth
⏰ Time-Based Sales Trends
Highest sales occur during the Morning Rush
Sales gradually decline throughout the day
Afternoon periods represent untapped revenue potential
☕ Product Performance
Coffee is the top-performing product category
Tea shows steady demand
Drinking Chocolate and other items underperform
🏪 Store Performance
Overall, stores show consistent and strong performance
Revenue is stable, with clear peak periods driving sales
📈 Key Metrics
Total Revenue
Total Transactions
Total Quantity Sold
Average Transaction Value (ATV)
Sales by Product Category
Sales by Time Interval
⚠️ Challenges Identified
Low sales during off-peak hours
Underperformance of certain products
Missed opportunities for upselling and promotions
💡 Recommendations
🔹 Improve Low-Performing Products
Bundle with popular items
Offer discounts and promotions
Improve product visibility and variety
🔹 Maximise Peak Hours
Optimise service speed during mornings
Introduce combo deals to increase transaction value
🔹 Boost Off-Peak Sales
Launch afternoon promotions
Offer time-based discounts
Introduce targeted marketing campaigns
🔹 Leverage Seasonality
Introduce winter-themed drinks and promotions
Capitalise on increased demand during colder months
